Our Menus

As the seasons change, so too does the availability of the ingredients we use in our food. We have no set dates for this and we never change the whole menu at once. Instead, the menu evolves as different produce from our Yorkshire farms becomes available to us.

Our tasting menu offers a selection of courses that James and the team believe best showcases the current harvest from the farm, alongside produce that’s been preserved throughout the year. Or for those with smaller appetites or limited time, an express menu is also available. Both the tasting and express menus can be enjoyed with an optional drinks package, served alongside courses.

Our express menu costs £60, or £100 with the optional drinks pairing.

Our tasting menu costs £100, or £170 with the optional drinks pairing.
